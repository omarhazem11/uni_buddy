Uni-Verse ("we", "our", "the App") is a productivity and university-discovery app for students in Egypt. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in using Google, Facebook, or Apple, we receive basic profile information from that provider, including:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile picture (if available)

We also collect information you create while using the App, including:

  • Tasks, due dates, priorities, and categories you create
  • Planner entries and schedules
  • Notes and tags you write
  • Achievement and progress data (badges, streaks, points)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the core features of the App (tasks, planner, notes, achievements)
  • To personalize your experience (e.g. greeting you by name, showing relevant reminders)
  • To improve the App and fix bugs
  • To communicate with you about updates or support requests
  • To display advertisements and offer premium features (see Section 6)

3. Third-Party Services

Uni-Verse uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and data storage
  • Google Sign-In, Facebook Login, Apple Sign-In — for account creation and login
  • Advertising and analytics partners (to be named here once selected)

These providers may process your data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase, with access restricted to your own account. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at [SUPPORT EMAIL]. We will process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Advertising & Subscriptions

Uni-Verse may display advertisements to free users and offer a premium subscription with additional features, including a free trial period. Subscription terms, pricing, and cancellation details are described in our Terms of Service.

7. Children's Privacy

Uni-Verse is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.
